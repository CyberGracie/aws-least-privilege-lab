# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a **welcoming and inspiring community** for all learners, regardless of background, experience level, or identity. This project is dedicated to education, and we pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- 🤝 **Being respectful** — Treat all community members with kindness
- 💡 **Being helpful** — Share knowledge generously; help others learn
- 🎓 **Being patient** — Remember everyone is learning at their own pace
- 🔍 **Being constructive** — Provide feedback that helps improve the lab
- 🙏 **Being inclusive** — Welcome perspectives from different backgrounds
- 📢 **Being open** — Listen to suggestions and new ideas

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- ❌ Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- ❌ Insulting or demeaning comments about background or experience level
- ❌ Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- ❌ Trolling, personal attacks, or dismissive comments
- ❌ Sharing others' private information without consent
- ❌ Deliberate disruption of discussions or meetings

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Comments on commits and code reviews
- Any interactions within this repository

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report it** — Email `cybergracie@example.com` or use GitHub's report feature
2. **Provide details** — Include context and specific examples
3. **Be safe** — Your safety and comfort come first

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. We are committed to addressing violations promptly and fairly.

## Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warning or reminder of community standards
- Temporary suspension from participation
- Removal from the community (in severe cases)

## Our Responsibility

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations
- Protecting the confidentiality of reporters
